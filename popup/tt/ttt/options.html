Addon compatibility check for the next ESR (TB 91)

The table shows the installed addons and their description, as well as their compatibility with the next ESR, TB 91.

If a * is given, the addon is either compatible with TB 91, or the addon author did not specify any max version of Thunderbird.

If the highest addon version on ATN is more recent than your installed version, then it is either incompatible with your version of Thunderbird, or its installation might be blocked. I personally had Kaspersky blocking addon updates.
