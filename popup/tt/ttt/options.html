Add-on compatibility check for the next ESR (TB 91)

The table shows the installed add-ons and their description, as well as their compatibility information.

If * is printed in the Compatibility column, the add-on is either compatible with TB 91, or the add-on author did not specify any max version of Thunderbird.

If the highest add-on version on ATN is more recent than your installed version, then it is either incompatible with your current version of Thunderbird, or its installation might be blocked. I personally had Kaspersky blocking add-on updates.
