Add-on info, addon-sync, updates and compatibility in next ESR (TB 91)

The table may need time for loading.

It shows the installed addons and their description, as well as compatibility with the next ESR, TB 91.

If a * is given, the addon is either compatible with TB 91, or the addon author did not specify any max version of Thunderbird.

If the max version of the addon on ATN is more recent than your installed version, then it is either incompatible with your version of Thunderbird, or its installation might be blocked. I personally had Kaspersky blocking addon updates.

You can print the table or copy it to clipboard. Paste it into Libreoffice or Excel to retrieve it as a formatted table.

If you email it to yourself, this or another Thunderbird installation can indicate those addons that are not installed.
For this to work, this addon must be installed in the other Thunderbird installation.
